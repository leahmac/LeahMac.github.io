Auntie Lizzie and her daughter Nanny, Nanny Lindsay, they lived in Girvan for years, in a very small house with gas lamps and an outdoor toilet. Nanny married the baker on Dalrymple Street, remember? She put her head in an oven, very sad. But in their garden was a gypsy caravan, a real one, all painted in Romany colours, and hung with ribbons and gypsy china. Such a place to play when we were girls. They say there were gypsies in our family you know, my Great Great Grandmother Creole was a fiery Spanish woman….it’s where my dark hair comes from, and your brother’s (and your temper, mutters my mother, under her breath).
